The Idea

An open market for the asset everyone sits on and no one can sell.

Five ways of looking at the same thesis: data, priced and traded like any other financial instrument.

About Us
Open Data Marketplace

A financial platform built on and for data, where datasets are listed, priced, and owned the way equity is.

The Problem
Billions sitting idle

Companies pay millions for data their competitors can buy too. No market exists to turn proprietary datasets into real financial assets.

Our Solution
A place where data can be sold and traded, legally

Buy a dataset outright, yours exclusively, forever, closed to every competitor. Or list your own data, sell shares in it, and take it public. Datic Currency is the stock market for data: buy, sell, trade and invest in datasets, including verified ESG reports built for MSMEs that had no affordable way to create or monetise one before.

What You Gain
Built to reward every side of the trade

For traders, it's a new asset class: transparent, liquid, and among the safest and most rewarding markets to enter early. For businesses sitting on unused data, it's a way to finally earn from what was dead weight. For businesses buying, it's lifetime exclusive ownership of the data they acquire, unreachable to any competitor.

Data Marketplace
Bid for exclusivity

A firm's dataset is listed on Datic Currency. Other firms bid for exclusive access, and the winning bid takes it.

Investment Platform
Public invests, price moves

The public buys data shares in a listed dataset. Bidding drives the share price. A 1.2% commission applies on every trade.
