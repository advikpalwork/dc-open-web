How It Works

A new financial primitive for the data economy.

01

List & Tokenise

A company securely lists a verified, anonymised dataset. We tokenise it into a fixed number of "data shares" — representing a stake in that series, which grows in value as new datasets are merged into it over time.

02

Bid for Insight

Corporate buyers compete in a transparent auction for the main 40% share block. To gain exclusive access to the underlying data, the winning bidder must acquire a majority stake (≥51%). Competitive bidding drives the price.

03

Trade & Invest

50% of shares are released to public investors on our live open market. A self-regulating, formula-driven pricing engine (α_dynamic) determines real-time value. Long-term holders earn as the dataset compounds in value.

04

Evolve & Earn

Data sellers earn from the auction, the public market, and future merges. Investors earn as demand rises. DC earns 1.2% on every buy and sell — a scalable, exchange-model revenue stream with no precedent.
