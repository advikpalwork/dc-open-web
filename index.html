Product & Service

How Datic Currency Works

A clear, transparent process — from the moment data is listed to the moment investors earn returns.

01
List & Tokenize
A company securely lists a non-personal dataset. We verify its quality and tokenize it into a fixed number of data shares. These shares represent a stake in the series, growing in value as the company merges new datasets over time — creating recurring cycles of demand and rewarding long-term holders.
02
Trade & Discover
50% of the shares are released to public investors. Trading on our open market discovers the real-time live price of the data — driven entirely by market demand, not arbitrary pricing. Anyone can participate in the public market.
03
Bid for Insight
Corporate bidders compete in a transparent auction to win the main 40% block of shares. To gain exclusive access to the underlying data, the winner must acquire a majority stake — ensuring serious, committed buyers only.
04
Evolve & Grow
Shares can be part of a Data Series, where new datasets are merged into existing shares. This transforms the asset from a one-time key into a living, long-term investment that grows in value with each update — like a stock that earns from ongoing company performance.

Financial Model

Diversified Revenue,
Built to Scale

A commission-based model designed for high margins and scalability — revenue grows directly with platform activity.

STREAM 01
Corporate Transaction Fees
A commission is charged to both the data seller and the winning corporate bidder at the conclusion of each auction — calculated as a percentage of the final auction price. Revenue scales with data value.
STREAM 02
Public Market Fees
A small percentage-based transaction fee applied to every trade on the public market. As trading volume grows with adoption, this stream compounds without additional overhead.
STREAM 03
Platform Service Fees
One-time listing fees for new Data Series, commissions on Platform Reserve sales, and service fees for the Resale Bank and Guaranteed Buy-Back program create a stable recurring base.
