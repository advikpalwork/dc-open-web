What is Datic Currency

Data Tokenized as
Tradeable Shares

Datic Currency (DC) is a financial framework that converts raw datasets into publicly tradeable shares — fairly priced, safely encrypted, and open to anyone to invest in.

  • 📊

    Data Becomes an Asset

    Any dataset — regional sales, usage metrics, strategic intelligence — can be listed, priced, and traded like equity shares on a public market.

  • ⚡

    Fair Market Pricing

    Prices are set by a transparent model that factors in data quality, recency, risk, and market demand — no guesswork, no negotiation.

  • 🔐

    Encryption Until Majority

    Your data stays fully encrypted and private until a buyer acquires 51% stake — protecting sellers while incentivising genuine competitive interest.

  • 💹

    Real-Time Price Movement

    Share prices adjust dynamically as demand rises. More bidders and higher trading volume push the price up — benefiting all shareholders.

How It Works

From Raw Data to
Traded Asset

Three simple stages take your dataset from private file to publicly traded instrument — with full protection at every step.

📋

List & Price

Submit your dataset and set a base price. Our transparent pricing model calculates a fair Initial Share Price based on the type of data, its age, quality, and regulatory compliance. Shares are minted instantly.

Open Auction

Corporate buyers bid over a 7-day window. Each bid must exceed the last by a minimum increment. As more companies compete, demand rises and the live share price moves upward — benefiting all public investors.

Security Protocol

Multi-Layer
Trust Architecture

Every dataset listed on Datic Currency passes through a rigorous trust assessment before shares can be issued. Data is never exposed until the majority unlock threshold is met.

⭐
Source Reputation Scoring
Providers are scored on their trustworthiness and regulatory compliance history. Higher reputation scores increase the dataset's overall Risk Factor — and its listing price.
✅
Data Completeness Validation
Datasets must pass completeness checks. Datasets with major gaps or inconsistencies receive lower scores, reducing their market value — and incentivising quality submissions.
🔒
Re-Identification Risk Assessment
Anonymous datasets are evaluated for the risk of tracing data back to individuals. Low re-identification risk earns a higher security score — protecting both subjects and sellers.
🔑
Majority Unlock Protocol
All datasets remain AES-256 encrypted until a buyer acquires 51% stake. Partial access is impossible — ensuring full commercial intent before any data is released.

Auction System

Live Bidding.
Algorithmic Fairness.

01

Auction Opens at Initial Price

Bidding begins at the algorithmically calculated Initial Share Price. All verified corporate entities are eligible to participate in the open window.

02

Incremental Bidding

Each new bid must exceed the previous by a minimum increment. This prevents bid clustering and ensures meaningful, progressive price discovery throughout the window.

03

Demand Drives Live Price

As bidder count, trade volume, and order book depth increase, the Demand Factor rises — pushing the live market price upward. Public shareholders benefit from every new bid.

04

7-Day Window Closes

At the end of the auction window, the highest bidder wins. If tied, Datic's 10% reserve breaks the deadlock by prioritising the longer-held position.

05

Majority Unlock & Settlement

The winner purchases ≥51% stake, triggering full dataset decryption and delivery. Returns are automatically distributed to all shareholders based on price appreciation.
